Past Work

Tactopus Learning Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Founder, CEO

Jan '17 - Jan '20 (3 yrs)

Tactopus is an inclusive education technology company I founded while in design school. Having witnessed that access to learning was severely limited for children with vision loss, I almost instantly saw a huge opportunity to develop tech-enabled tools to make learning more accessible and fun.

Tactopus is now a team of ten, with products in 50+ schools and resource centres for children with disabilities, and customers in 7 countries.

I’ve done what founders typically do- a little and lot of nearly everything. That includes design, prototyping, user-testing, service design, interaction design, unity development, web development, design for manufacturing, illustrations, product-market fit, business development, marketing, supply chain, sales, distributor deals, fund-raising, stakeholder management, hiring, and organisation design.

Grants received: $65K
Revenue from sales: $40K
Current valuation: $1.2M
