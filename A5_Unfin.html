Technical Illustrations for the Boook
"The Unfinished: The Stone Carvers at Work in the Indian Subcontinent"

Amazon Link

When I was a student at NIT Trichy, I got the opoprtunity to work with Prof. Vidya Dehejia and Scupltor Peter Rockwell. The assignment was to create technical illustrations for the book they were working on, to decipher the stone scultipng technology and process by stydying rock cut temples and other samples of stone carvers' work in various stages of completion.

Above: The team, Geslin George, Kumar Abhishek and me, with the authors.

It was a rewardign experience to work with eminent historians, and the job involved travel, drawing, hearing a lot about the history we were supposed to represent in the drawings. Of course, really proud of the drawings making it to the final publication.
