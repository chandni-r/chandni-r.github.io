Tactopus: Inclusive, Multi-sensory Learning

I founded Tactopus while in design school, with a mission to address the lack of access to learning material for children with disabilities.
Thanks to the support of many individuals and organisations, we are now a fully-operational team of ten, serving schools, resource centres, parents and children with disabilities all over the world.
