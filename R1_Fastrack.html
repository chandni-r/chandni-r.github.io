Project Management for Fastrack New Retail Identity

My first assignment upon joining Titan Company, was to work with Lotus Design.
I was responsible for working with the project team on site, fittings manufactuing vendors, brand marketing teams and the architects.
Sourcing material for this quirky and irreverent store was a challenge but also fun.
It was also a good way to learn all about retail projects, it was a little like being pushed into the deep end of the pool.

Learning: project management, exposure to returns on investment, collaboration, working with 100+ individual contributors, vendors, contractors, delivering project on schedule (of 90 days), managing conflicts, offering practical decision making information to design and brand teams based on project progress, cost management.

Also personal stress management, leadership development with a team that was culturally and economically diverse, learnt to speak Hindi more fluently during this time, as I had to work with a largely hindi-speaking workforce on site. I was put on this project and guided by Mr. Kulothungan, senior management who was ex-CRPF, with a career in sales. He had only veteran-standard expectations from my work and I learnt to imbibe that in my process.
